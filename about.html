About Us

Who are we?

We are Smile of Hope African Ministries

SMILE OF HOPE AFRICA MINISTRIES (SOHAM) is a community-based organization operating in Uganda. The ministry was founded by Pastor AKILIMALI DANANY and his wife GRACE BUNTU, the goal is to fulfil the great commission (Mathew 28:16-20) through clear proclamation of the gospel and the demonstration of God’s Love through his Son Jesus in word but also in deeds and by God’s grace bringing smiles and hope in the lives of individuals and communities struggling due to different circumstances in Uganda and beyond.

SOHAM operate through and with different local churches and other like minded organizations to fulfill its mission and vision, by focusing on planting and revitalizing churches, equipping church leaders, building community bible study groups, education,entrepreneurship, skill development, children sponsorship, counseling, and the demonstration of God’s love and care among others.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to share the gospel in our communities in words and actions by equipping disciples, empowering individuals with skills, entrepreneurship, health promotion, farming, strengthen the communities and other community based programs for the praise of His glorious name.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see Christ exalted, a world of hope and smile and overcoming material and spiritual poverty and living transformed lives.

Core Values.

Christ-centered

"Christ-centered" means making Jesus Christ the focal point of all beliefs and actions, guided by his principles of love, compassion, humility, and service.

God glorifying

"God glorifying" means living in a way that honors and brings praise to God, reflecting His greatness and holiness in all actions and decisions. It involves being dedicated to serving God and making His presence known in the world.

Compassionate

"Compassionate" means showing genuine concern and empathy for others, and taking action to alleviate their suffering. It involves kindness, understanding, and a deep desire to help those in need.

Faithful

"Faithful" means being loyal and steadfast in one's beliefs and commitments, especially in adhering to one's faith and principles. It involves trustworthiness, reliability, and unwavering devotion to God and His teachings.

Truthful

"Truthful" means being honest and transparent in all interactions and communications. It involves consistently telling the truth and upholding integrity in every aspect of life.

Servant-hearted

"Servant-hearted" means having a genuine desire to serve others selflessly, prioritizing their needs and well-being above one's own. It involves humility, compassion, and a commitment to making a positive impact in the lives of others.

impact-driven in everything we do.

"Impact-driven" means focusing on creating meaningful and positive changes in everything we do. It involves being dedicated to making a significant and measurable difference in the lives of individuals and communities.

Integrity

"Integrity" means adhering to strong moral and ethical principles, being honest and trustworthy in all actions. It involves consistently doing the right thing, even when no one is watching, and maintaining a commitment to truth and fairness.

Accountability

"Accountability" means being responsible for one's actions and decisions, and being willing to explain and accept the consequences. It involves transparency, ownership, and a commitment to following through on promises and obligations.

Discipleship

"Discipleship" means the process of learning, growing, and following the teachings of Jesus Christ. It involves mentoring, nurturing faith, and guiding others to become devoted followers of Christ through study, prayer, and community involvement.
